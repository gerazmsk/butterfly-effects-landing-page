What Is ABA Therapy and How It Helps Children With Autism

Butterfly Effects is a leading provider of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy for children with autism spectrum disorder. Our evidence-based approach focuses on teaching meaningful skills and reducing challenging behaviors using positive reinforcement and individualized treatment plans.

At Butterfly Effects, we understand that every child is unique. Our Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) create personalized programs that help children:

  • Improve communication and language
  • Build social and play skills
  • Reduce challenging behaviors
  • Increase independence
  • Support learning and school readiness

With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, Butterfly Effects has helped thousands of families across the country. Our Deerfield Beach center brings this same high-quality, compassionate care to families in Broward County.

After completing the intake form and insurance verification, your child can typically begin ABA therapy within 2-4 weeks. We work efficiently to get services started as soon as possible.

We provide ABA therapy for children of all ages, from early intervention (toddlers) through school-age children and adolescents. Our programs are tailored to each child's developmental stage and needs.

Yes, we specialize in early intervention ABA therapy for young children. Early intervention is crucial for optimal outcomes, and our BCBAs are experienced in working with toddlers and preschoolers.

An ABA assessment includes a comprehensive evaluation of your child's skills, behaviors, and needs. A licensed BCBA will conduct observations, parent interviews, and standardized assessments to create a personalized treatment plan.

The number of ABA therapy hours varies based on your child's individual needs, as determined during the assessment. Typical programs range from 10-40 hours per week, depending on the child's age, goals, and insurance authorization.

Yes, we offer center-based ABA therapy at our Deerfield Beach location. Our center provides a structured, supportive environment with dedicated therapy rooms and resources designed for children with autism.

Yes, we provide in-home ABA therapy services throughout Broward County. In-home therapy allows children to learn in their natural environment, which can be beneficial for generalization of skills.

ABA therapy helps improve a wide range of skills including communication, language, social interactions, play skills, daily living skills, academic readiness, and reduction of challenging behaviors.

Parent involvement is essential to successful ABA therapy. We provide regular parent training sessions, progress updates, and strategies you can use at home to support your child's development and generalize skills.

We use data-driven methods to track progress, including regular assessments, behavior tracking, skill acquisition data, and parent feedback. Progress reports are shared regularly with families.

Our team consists of Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) who are licensed professionals, and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) who are certified and supervised by BCBAs. All staff undergo ongoing training and certification.
